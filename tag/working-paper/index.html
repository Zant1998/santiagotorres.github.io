The Oracle Local Polynomial Estimator
This paper introduces a new estimator for continuity-based Regression Discontinuity (RD) designs named the estimated Oracle Local Polynomial Estimator (OLPE), a new consistent estimator of the target causal effect exhibiting lower bias and/or variance than traditional local polynomial estimators.
