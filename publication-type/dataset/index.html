A Historical Characterization of Colombian Political Parties: 1958-2022
This dataset classifies all political parties, movements, and coalitions have competed in elections for mayors, municipal councilors, assembly deputies, governors, lower house representatives, senators, and presidents in Colombia for which there are records into five dimensions: their ideology, the identity groups they represent, their traditionality, their degree of nationalization, and longevity.
Nicolás Cabra-Ruíz, Santiago Torres, Laura Wills-Otero, Valentina Castilla-Gutiérrez

Colombia’s Electoral Results
This repository is comprised of 124 datasets containing the results of the vast majority of elections for mayors, municipal councilors, assembly deputies, governors, lower house representatives, senators, and presidents held in Colombia between 1958 and 2022. Moreover, we present a new database containing information on all of the political parties, movements, coalitions, and significant citizen groups that were involved in these elections.
Santiago Torres, Andrés Barinas-Forero, Wilson Forero-Mesa, Juan Ernesto Sanchez, Mercedes Tibavisco
